# Product Context

## Game Overview

**Cosmic Cults** is a Lovecraftian 4X real-time strategy game featuring:

- **Three playable cults** with asymmetric mechanics
- **Isometric diorama view** (FF7-style bounded scenes)
- **Hex-based tactical combat** with real-time unit control
- **Fog of war** and exploration mechanics
- **Procedurally generated** units and terrain elements

## Target Audience

- Strategy game enthusiasts
- Lovecraft/cosmic horror fans
- Mobile gamers seeking deeper experiences
- Players who enjoy asymmetric faction gameplay

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. **Explore** - Scout the map, reveal fog of war
2. **Expand** - Build structures, claim territory
3. **Exploit** - Gather resources, convert followers
4. **Exterminate** - Engage enemy cults, corrupt/sanctify territory

## Faction Overview

### Void Seekers (Purple)
- Focus on stealth and manipulation
- Units phase through terrain
- Win by reaching cosmic enlightenment

### Flesh Weavers (Pink/Red)
- Focus on numbers and mutation
- Units can merge and split
- Win by total biomass domination

### Star Children (Gold/White)
- Focus on purity and cleansing
- Units sanctify corrupted land
- Win by purifying all eldritch corruption

## Visual Identity

- **Cel-shaded** 3D graphics with outlined edges
- **Isometric** orthographic camera
- **Eldritch** effects: tentacles, eyes, cosmic particles
- **Color-coded** faction territories and units

## Mobile-First Design

- **Touch optimized** controls (tap-select, drag-pan)
- **Session length** ~15-30 minute matches
- **Portrait and landscape** support
- **Offline capable** single-player campaign

## Success Metrics

- 60 FPS on Pixel 8a
- <3s cold start to gameplay
- Complete match in 15-30 minutes
- Zero P0 bugs at launch
